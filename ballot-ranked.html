Voting Instructions

Review all 27 applicants and rank your top 15 from 1 (highest priority) to 15 (lowest priority among your choices).

You may rank fewer than 15 if you prefer. Applicants you don't rank receive no support from your ballot.

How points are awarded: Your rankings are converted to points in reverse order. The applicant you rank #1 receives 15 points. Rank #2 = 14 points, #3 = 13 points, and so on, down to rank #15 = 1 point. Applicants you don't rank receive 0 points from your ballot. Points from all voters are summed to determine funding priority.
