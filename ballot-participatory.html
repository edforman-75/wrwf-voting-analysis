You have 100 points to distribute across the 27 applicants below. Every member receives the same 100 points regardless of contribution level.

Allocate any number of points to any applicant. You may give all 100 to one organization, spread them across many, or anything in between.

Each applicant's requested grant amount is shown. The tracker at the top shows how many points you've used and how many remain.

What's different: Instead of abstract rankings, you're making concrete allocation decisions. Giving 20 points to a $5,000 request is the same commitment as giving 20 points to a $25,000 request ‚Äî but the smaller organization needs fewer total points across all voters to be fully funded. The tradeoffs are explicit: every point you give one applicant is a point you can't give another.
