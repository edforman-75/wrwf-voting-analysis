Applicants are grouped by grant size into three tiers. Within each tier, rank your top 5 choices from 1 (highest priority) to 5.

Key improvement: Prevents large-request organizations from absorbing all the funding. Each tier has its own allocation, ensuring diversity in grant sizes.
